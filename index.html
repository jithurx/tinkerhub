Who Are We?

We're the NSSCE chapter of the TinkerHub Foundation! A vibrant community driven by a passion for technology, innovation, and collaborative learning. We bring together students who share an enthusiasm for tinkering, making, coding, and building cool things. We believe in learning through experimentation, sharing knowledge openly, supporting each other, and fostering a welcoming environment where ideas can flourish. Whether you're a coding newbie or a seasoned maker, there's a place for you here. Join the organized chaos and let's create the future, together!
